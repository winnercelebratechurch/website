Growing Together in Faith

We are a community of ordinary people seeking to know an extraordinary God. Whether you've been a believer for decades or are just starting to ask questions about faith, there is a place for you here.

Our mission in Winner is simple: Love God, Love People, and Serve the World. We believe in authentic community, practical teaching, and passionate worship.
